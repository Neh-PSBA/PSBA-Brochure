Doorstep Banking Services - Deep Dive

Problems Solved for Banks:

  • Expanding Customer Reach: Effectively serves segments like senior citizens, divyangjan individuals, and those in remote locations without needing to expand physical branches.

  • Operational Efficiency & Resource Optimization: Streamlines routine banking services, freeing up branch staff for higher-value tasks.

  • Cost Savings: Reduces pressure on branch infrastructure and avoids CAPEX/OPEX involved in opening or maintaining additional branches.

  • Enhanced Compliance: Supports financial inclusion and accessibility, meeting key regulatory requirements from the RBI.

  • Uniform Service Delivery: Banks leverage a shared infrastructure model, reducing implementation effort while ensuring consistency across geographies.

Key Features:

  • Broad Range of Services: Includes Cash Withdrawal & Deposit, Cheque Pick-up, Demand Draft Request, Life Certificate Submission (especially for pensioners), KYC Document Pick-up, and Form 15G/15H Collection.

  • Secure, Tracked, and Verified Transactions: Ensures security with OTP-based customer authentication, mobile app-enabled real-time tracking, and eReceipts/automated service logs for a full audit trail.

  • Robust Technology Stack: Centralized platform accessible by all partner banks, with integration-ready APIs and mobile workforce management (geo-tagging, time-stamped logs).

  • Ease for Bank Staff: Provides a dashboard for service monitoring and MIS reports, an intuitive service request interface, and escalation workflows for issue resolution.

  • Pan-India Network: Leverages a shared agent network across locations with standardized training and branding for all field agents.

Customer Benefit:

Ultimate Convenience and Inclusion. Banking that comes to you — safely, securely, and at your doorstep. No need to physically visit a bank branch, especially critical for senior citizens, differently-abled individuals, and rural/remote customers. Services are provided at the comfort of home without compromising on security or authenticity.

Benefits Unlocked with Doorstep Banking:

Time Saved: No more waiting in queues.

Effortless Access: Banking from your home.

Enhanced Security: Verified transactions.

Inclusion for All: No barriers to banking.

Baanknet.com - Deep Dive

Problems Solved for Banks:

  • NPA Recovery Optimization: Traditional recovery channels are fragmented, opaque, and slow. Baanknet accelerates asset liquidation and NPA resolution via a unified digital auction platform.

  • Transparency and Compliance: Manual and localized auction processes often lack audit trails. Baanknet ensures end-to-end traceability and regulatory compliance (e.g., SARFAESI, IBC norms).

  • Wider Market Reach: Auctions are often limited to local advertising or single-bank efforts. Baanknet aggregates listings from multiple banks, boosting visibility to national buyers and investors.

  • Operational Efficiency: Managing auctions is resource-heavy for bank staff. Baanknet digitizes the auction lifecycle, reducing manual workload, errors, and TATs.

Key Features:

  • Diverse Asset Listings: Residential, commercial, industrial properties, vehicles, agricultural land, warehouses, and movable assets from multiple PSBs and FIs, not just one bank.

  • Smart Auction Engine: Real-time bidding with auto-bid options, date scheduling, bidder management, result declaration, dynamic reserve pricing, and multi-lot configurations.

  • Security & Legal Compliance: Secured data exchange and user access, compliant with DRT, SARFAESI, Insolvency, and SEBI e-auction norms.

  • Bank-Facing Admin Portal: Intuitive dashboards for asset listing, auction configuration, document uploads, access controls, approval workflows, and MIS reports.

  • Buyer Interface: Seamless discovery and filtering by location, asset class, price, auction date, with alerts and reminders, real-time participation, and downloadable documents.

Digital Balance Confirmation Portal (DBCP) - Deep Dive

Problems DBCP Solves for Banks and Auditors:

  • Manual Dispatches & Follow-ups: Eliminates letters or emails manually sent, delayed, or missed, streamlining the process.

  • Data Entry Errors: Mitigates inconsistencies and version mismatches caused by manual filling.

  • Time-Consuming Processes: Reduces auditor waiting times and bank manpower spent chasing replies.

  • SA 505 Compliance Gaps: Ensures manual processes meet the standards of external confirmations as per SA 505.

  • Lack of Audit Trail: Provides clear verification of response receipt, modification, and authorized origination.

Key Features:

For Banks:
  • Single Dashboard to receive & respond to all confirmation requests
  • Custom Authorization Matrix for internal validations
  • Automated Workflow & MIS Reporting
For Auditors:
  • Digital Request Creation using official credentials
  • Real-Time Tracking of request status (sent, acknowledged, responded)
  • Immutable Audit Trail ensuring non-repudiation
For All:
  • End-to-End Encryption
  • Access Control & Role-Based Permissions
  • Integration-Ready APIs for core banking and audit systems

Primary Benefit for End Customers (Businesses/Clients of Banks):

DBCP significantly reduces disruption to end businesses during audit season by offering:

  • No Need for Physical Coordination: Eliminates manual back-and-forth.
  • Faster Closure of Audits: Expedites the overall audit process.
  • Accuracy in Responses: Ensures reliable data due to structured digital inputs.
  • Confidence in Compliance: Helps meet standards and timelines efficiently.

PSBXCHANGE (Digital Supply Chain Finance) - Deep Dive

Problems Solved for Banks:

  • Low SCF Penetration: Addresses the limited SCF offerings by PSBs through a ready digital platform tailored for public banks.

  • Operational Inefficiency: Eliminates repetitive integration efforts with multiple fintechs by offering a single gateway.

  • Legacy Tech Limitations: Offers a modern digital tech stack that overcomes outdated systems.

  • Limited Market Reach: Leverages fintech tie-ups to access micro-markets and underserved tiers (2 & 3).

  • Lack of Real-Time Insight: Enables performance tracking of fintech partnerships, increasing visibility and accountability.

Problems Solved for Businesses (Suppliers/Buyers/MSMEs):

  • Liquidity Gaps: Immediate access to working capital through invoice-based lending options (DF, VF, PID, SID).

  • Slow Financing Cycles: Digitization enables faster approvals and disbursals.

  • Limited Access to Banks: Aggregated access to multiple banks through a single platform.

  • Document and Compliance Burden: Reduces paperwork through seamless digital onboarding and document submission (bank statements, GST, CIBIL, MCA data, etc.).

Key Features:

  • Comprehensive SCF Product Suite: DF, VF, PID, SID, Reverse Factoring – all in one platform.

  • Multi-Fintech Aggregator: Access to several fintech partners via single integration.

  • Real-Time Dashboard: Tracks performance, onboarding status, and transactions.

  • Customer-Consented Data Access: Auto-fetch bank statements, GST, CIBIL, MCA data securely.

  • ERP-Compatible & Scalable: Potential integration with client ERPs for transaction syncing.

  • Technology & Training Support: Provides best practices and PSB-specific SCF training modules.

Primary Benefit for End Businesses (MSMEs):

Fast and easy access to affordable working capital, powered by digital verification, lower turnaround time, and multiple financiers on one platform—resulting in better cash flow and business continuity.

Lower cost of capital

Increase in SCF transaction volume for banks
